=== Slide 22 ===
Unvarnished Truth: Migrations
What to do about it
Reality check
•  Code is the hard part.
Legacy warehouses hide decades of proprietary SQL, undocumented stored procedures, and tool-specific ETL. It won't convert 1:1 on day one.
•  Data movement has real limits.
Snapshot-style JDBC moves don't scale infinitely. Network throughput and source DW concurrency are real bottlenecks.
•  Big-bang migrations fail.
Rewrites across BI, ETL, and data models at once almost always slip schedule. Phased is the only approach that works.
•  Make risk visible first.
Use Lakebridge Profiler and Analyzer to inventory objects, measure complexity, and estimate effort before committing timelines.
•  Automate the repeatable 80%.
Lakebridge converts SQL/ETL and validates data (90%+ for Redshift/Synapse). Humans focus on the 20%.
•  Migrate in waves.
Start with 1-2 domains. Use Lakehouse Federation + Lakeflow connect to keep the old DW online while you cut over.
•  Set honest timelines.
Migrations run into unforeseen issues
. Tell customers: plan 3-
4
months per domain.

=== Slide 7 ===
The operational database built for AI apps and agents
Product Understanding
What It Is:
Lakebase Postgres is a new category of operational database built on the lake for AI era workloads. Lakebase separates compute and storage. Compute is Postgres and storage is lakehouse, so teams can scale instantly, control costs, and build on a single source of truth.
Why It Matters:
AI applications generate unpredictable, "spiky" traffic and require fresh data instantly. Traditional databases force you to choose between performance, cost, and integration. Lakebase removes these tradeoffs, providing a serverless engine that scales automatically and unifies your data so you can build intelligent apps without limits.
3 Key "So That" Statements
Serverless Autoscaling:
Lakebase scales compute up instantly when busy and scales down to zero when idle...
so that
you stop paying for "always-on" resources and drastically reduce TCO for variable workloads and AI agents.
Instant Database Branching:
Enable instant database branching, so that teams can develop, test, and experiment in isolation without copying data or risking production.
Standard Open Source Postgres:
Lakebase is 100% standard Postgres under the hood...
so that
your developers can instantly use their favorite tools and frameworks with zero learning curve and no proprietary vendor lock-in.
